Caregiver churn has reached 60% or higher in some agencies, putting staffing, client relationships, and margins at risk. Home-based care organizations are under pressure to do more with less, but many are still asking caregivers to manage complex, fragmented workflows on outdated tools. A caregiver-first mobile caregiver platform changes that reality.
